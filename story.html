We help connect those who need help with those who can

Do me a solid started off as the frustrations of a tourist in a foreign town. Every day there are millions of travellers all across the globe who are in the same situation of just needing a bit of help with something. There are plenty of services out there that can help but they cost an arm and a leg to hire, not to mention the hardest part - looking who to find

Hiring services such as photographers or interpreters can be a costly process - both in time and money. You'll need to know agencies in the area, which ones have the best deals, and most importantly if you can trust them or not. Even if you were willing to do all this, why would you hire a professional photographer just to follow you around for a day or two to take photos of you on a holiday?

So what if you could just get someone to come down for a little bit and help you out in exchange for a tip of sorts. Someone to pop on by and "Do you a solid" and be recognised for their effort? And because this someone isn't a world class photographer or interpreter with more degrees than a thermometer - it won't cost you your trip and then some.

What can you do for us?

What we do is simple - find people who need help, and connect them with people who can help.
